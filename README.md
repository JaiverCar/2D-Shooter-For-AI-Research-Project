This was a final project for our 'AI For Games' class at DigiPen Institute of Technology.

To open it, you just need to unzip it and open the project in Unity!

The project showcases a 'Hive Mind' AI system for all the enemies in the game. You need to kill their main tower to stop the clock, but they will be increasing the timer by 
capturing your flag and scoring it at the tower. Try to get the lowest time!
